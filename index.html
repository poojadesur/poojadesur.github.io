Detecting Coordinated Boosting Attacks on App Reviews

Pooja Desur, collaborators

Built large-scale fraud detection pipelines on 319K+ Google Play reviews (301K users), modeling activity as a dynamic bipartite graph and developing a temporal anomaly detection framework to identify coordinated boosting attacks. Transformer-based Coreference Resolution & Entity Linking

Pooja Desur, collaborators

Led NLP research on transformer-based coreference resolution, building scalable training and evaluation pipelines to improve contextual entity linking and structured information extraction in long-form regional-language text. Achieved SOTA evaluation scores (ConLL, MUC, LEA, B3) with a combined score of ~68%; published at LREC-COLING ’24.

📑 Paper (LREC-COLING ’24)

Engineered a multi-modal analysis framework combining transformer-based NLP, lexicon-based sentiment analysis, acoustic feature extraction (Spotify API), and topic modeling to study communication during crisis periods. The study was published at Social Informatics ’22.
